Technology Incubation and Development of Entrepreneurs (TIDE) centre at UoH was initiated in the year 2012 with support of Department of Electronics and Information technology (DEITy), Ministry of Information Technology, Govt of India and University of Hyderabad. ASPIRE-TIDE aims to assist & encourage the research and scientific community at UoH to initiate technology start-up companies for commercial exploitation of technologies developed at UoH and elsewhere.

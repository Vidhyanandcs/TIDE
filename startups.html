Incubation Process


Students and prospective incuabtees are encouraged give presentations on their ideas and use the premises and facilities of ASPIRE-TIDE for brainstorming and proof of concept. Applicants with ready proof of concept are encouraged to register companies. A selection committee which includes external experts examines the proposal both technical and business models and makes recommendations regarding incubation. The start-up then enters into “agreement of incubation” with ASPIRE-TIDE. Seedfund support is subject to availability and based on recommendation of an expert committee.
